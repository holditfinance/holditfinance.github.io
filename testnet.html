Swapping Guide On Testnet

How to swap $BNB to $HOLDEM via Pancakeswap on Testnet

Step

1

Install and setup MetaMask

Install MetaMask (a browser extension or mobile app cryptocurrency wallet). After the installation, you need to connect MetaMask to BSC Testnet. (Click here for a step-by-step tutorial)

Step

2

Get free BNB to make transactions

Next, let’s get some funds to play with. Head over to BSC Testnet Faucet and paste your wallet address in the form to get free BNB.

Step

3

Swap $BNB to $HOLDEM

Head over to PancakeSwap for Testnet and paste the contract address of Holdem. Set the slippage tolerance to 13% - 14% (increase it if needed).
